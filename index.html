welcome to skyGram! the purpose of this app is to show off your best picture that captures the beauty of the sky. whether its a nice cloudy scene, sunset or sunrise, upload it here on the app and to show it off to the world.


1.) Begin By Clicking MySkyGram tab, from there select the camera option to snap your shot

2.) the location button allows you to identify the coordinates of where you took your picture for others to see and check it on their own

3.) after uploading your photo, write a caption that best fits the moment!
